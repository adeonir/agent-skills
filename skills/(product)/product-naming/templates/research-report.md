## Guidelines

- TOP PICK: Flag only one name as the strongest option when there is a clear winner
- Quality Score: Use [Good] / [Fair] / [Poor] for each criterion
- Risk: Keep it brief but specific -- mention concrete issues (e.g., "too close to competitor X", "pronunciation ambiguous in EN")
- Variations: Include 2-4 per top candidate; skip for weaker candidates
- Eliminated: Group all eliminated names in one table at the end
- No availability data in this report -- that belongs in the validation report
- Evaluate entry point: skip Competitor Landscape and Variations sections; keep Shortlist with quality scoring and Eliminated
